# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and code reviews.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Focus feedback on code, data, and ideas rather than people.
- Be inclusive and professional in all project spaces.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers at dominik.hladik@seznam.cz. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
